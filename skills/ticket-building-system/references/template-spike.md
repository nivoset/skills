# Spike / Investigation Template

**Suggested tag:** `Spike`

Time-boxed research or validation work. May optionally link to a parent Feature, decision, or affected area.

```markdown
## Question to Answer
[What specific question(s) must be answered?]

## Context
[Why is this investigation needed? What decision depends on the outcome?]

## Approach
1. [Investigation step 1]
2. [Investigation step 2]
3. [Continue as needed]

## Time Box
[Maximum allowed: e.g., "4 hours", "1 day"]

## Success Criteria
[When is this spike complete? What evidence satisfies the question?]

## Expected Deliverables
- [ ] Written recommendation or decision
- [ ] [Proof of concept / prototype / analysis doc / etc.]

## Follow-Up
[What ticket(s) should be created based on findings? Leave blank until spike completes.]
```
